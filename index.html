Features

Everything healthcare needs

A complete ecosystem for patients, doctors, and hospital administrators.

📅

Smart Appointment Booking

Book appointments with verified doctors across 500+ hospitals. Real-time slot availability with instant confirmation.

🤖

AI Symptom Checker

Describe your symptoms and get instant AI-powered guidance on which specialist you need to see.

📹

Video Consultations

High-quality video calls with your doctor from the comfort of home. Powered by Zego UIKit.

📋

Digital Prescriptions

Doctors issue digital prescriptions. Patients can view, download, and share them as PDF anytime.

🔒

Biometric Login

Secure fingerprint authentication for instant, password-free access to your health data.

💳

Razorpay Payments

Pay consultation fees securely through integrated Razorpay. Supports UPI, cards, and net banking.
